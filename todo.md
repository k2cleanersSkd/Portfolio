## Project Completion Summary

The K2 Cleaner & Renovators website has been successfully built with the following features:

### Backend Infrastructure
- **Database Schema**: MySQL tables for bookings and contact inquiries with full audit trails
- **tRPC Procedures**: Secure backend procedures for booking submissions and contact form handling
- **Notification System**: Owner notifications triggered on every booking inquiry or contact form submission
- **Authentication**: Manus OAuth integration for secure user management

### Frontend Design & Layout
- **Professional Branding**: Navy blue (#1a2e6b) and green (#2e8b3a) color scheme with modern typography
- **Sticky Navigation**: Persistent navbar with company logo, navigation links, and WhatsApp CTA
- **Hero Section**: Animated headline with trust badges (eco-friendly, insured, certified) and key stats (500+ clients, 5-star rating, 3+ years)

### Service Offerings (All 8 Services)
1. Home Cleaning - PKR 3,000
2. Office Cleaning - PKR 4,000
3. Hotel/Guest House - PKR 5,000
4. Water Tank Cleaning - PKR 2,500
5. Sofa & Carpet Cleaning - PKR 2,000
6. Post-Construction Cleaning - PKR 6,000
7. Renovation & Painting - PKR 8,000
8. Pest Control - PKR 3,500

### Key Sections
- **About Us**: Company story with 3+ years in business, 1000+ jobs completed, 500+ happy clients, all of GB service areas
- **Pricing Band**: Transparent starting prices for each service with "Free Inspection" callout
- **Why Choose Us**: 6 feature cards (Eco-Friendly Products, Trained Staff, Free Inspection, Satisfaction Guarantee, Affordable Pricing, Serving All of GB)
- **How It Works**: 4-step process (Call/WhatsApp, Free Inspection & Quote, We Do the Job, Pay After You're Happy)
- **Testimonials**: 6 customer reviews with 5-star ratings from real locations in Skardu and GB
- **Contact Form**: Full contact form with name, email, phone, subject, and message fields
- **Contact CTA**: Multiple booking options with phone, WhatsApp, email, location, and working hours
- **Footer**: Complete footer with all 8 services, quick links, contact details, and service areas

### Technical Features
- **Animations**: Framer Motion animations for sections, cards, and interactive elements
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first design that works on all devices
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct WhatsApp booking links throughout the site
- **Form Validation**: Client-side and server-side form validation
- **Error Handling**: User-friendly error messages and success notifications
- **Performance**: Optimized images, lazy loading, and efficient animations

### Service Areas Covered
- Skardu City, Kachura, Shigar, Satpara, Khaplu, Ghanche, Roundu, Shyok Valley, and surrounding areas of Gilgit-Baltistan

All requirements have been met and the website is ready for production deployment.
